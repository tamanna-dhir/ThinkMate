About ThinkMate

Your intelligent chess partner that learns, adapts, and grows with you.

Our Mission

ThinkMate is built to make chess more intuitive and intelligent. Powered by AI, it analyzes your games, predicts optimal moves, and helps you improve your strategy like never before. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, ThinkMate aims to make your gameplay insightful, challenging, and rewarding.

Core Features

AI Coach

Receive move suggestions, explanations, and game insights powered by machine learning.

Multiplayer

Challenge your friends or global players in real-time matches.

Puzzles

Sharpen your tactical understanding through thousands of AI-selected puzzles.

Player vs ThinkMate

Play against our AI engine, designed to simulate human-like strategies and adapt to your skill level.
